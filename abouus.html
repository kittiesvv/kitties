About Us

Learn about our mission, vision, and the dedicated team at Kitties Pad Rescue.

Our Mission

Providing Compassionate Care and Preventing Animal Cruelty

At Kitties Pad Rescue, our mission is to rescue, rehabilitate, and re-home abandoned, abused, and neglected animals. We strive to provide a safe and loving environment for every animal in our care, while promoting responsible pet ownership and advocating for animal welfare within our community.

Our Vision

A Community Where Every Animal is Safe and Loved

We envision a future where all animals are treated with compassion and respect. Our goal is to create a community where every animal has a loving home, free from cruelty and neglect. We work tirelessly towards this vision through our rescue efforts, adoption services, and community outreach programs.

Our Values

Guiding Principles of Our Work

Compassion

We believe in treating every animal with kindness, empathy, and respect, regardless of their background or condition.

Dedication

We are committed to providing the highest quality care for every animal, ensuring their physical and emotional well-being.

Community

We believe in the power of community and work collaboratively with partners, volunteers, and supporters to achieve our mission.

Our History

A Pawsitive Impact on the Community

  • 2015

    Founded on Compassion

    Kitties Pad Rescue was founded by a group of animal lovers committed to making a difference in the lives of homeless and neglected animals.

  • 2018

    Expanding Our Reach

    We launched our foster care program, enabling us to care for more animals and provide them with the individual attention they deserve.

  • 2021

    Building a Stronger Future

    We secured a grant to expand our facilities and enhance our medical care capabilities, allowing us to help even more animals in need.

  • Today

    Continuing Our Mission

    We remain dedicated to rescuing, rehabilitating, and re-homing animals, while advocating for responsible pet ownership and promoting animal welfare in our community.
