About Kitties Pad Rescue

Our mission is to prevent cruelty to animals and provide compassionate care.

Kitties Pad Rescue is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization dedicated to animal protection and welfare. We provide a sanctuary for abandoned, abused, and neglected animals, offering them a second chance at a happy and healthy life.

Your support is vital to our mission. Through donations, fostering, volunteering, or adopting, you can help us provide essential care, medical treatment, and loving homes for animals in need. Thank you for joining us in creating a brighter future for these deserving creatures.

Our Services

Comprehensive Animal Care Solutions

Rescue Operations

We conduct rescue operations for abandoned and abused animals, providing immediate safety and care.

Foster Care

Our foster care program provides temporary homes for animals, giving them the love and attention they need.

Medical Care

We provide necessary medical treatments, vaccinations, and spay/neuter services to ensure the health of our animals.

Adoption Services

We facilitate the adoption process, connecting loving families with animals in need of forever homes.

Donation Programs

Support our mission through monetary donations, supplies, or by sponsoring an animal's care.

Volunteer Opportunities

Join our team of dedicated volunteers and help us make a difference in the lives of animals.
